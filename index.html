USERPOOLS

Rewarding the match makers in the network with the tokens

Pools can be visualized as a cross between Slack channels and Meetup groups- federated, invite-only groups owned and run by devoted matchmakers. In this paper, we will describe the process by which matchmakers are rewarded in the network’s internal token for matching new entrants successfully. The quality or reputation of particular pools is measured against whether the joiner’s particular goals have been met.

DISCOVERY

Match matching goes
beyond than just dating

It doesn’t end with matching people for love. Matchmaking can be done in any domain, like matching professionals, matching artists, matching business that compliment each other, etc. In short bloomatch is trying to create a platform protocol on which other businesses can also build their use cases by altering and optimising over the protocol making the match algorithm more efficient and evolve as it goes into public.
